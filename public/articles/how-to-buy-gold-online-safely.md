## 1. Investing in a Gold IRA Online

A Gold IRA (Individual Retirement Account) lets you hold physical gold inside a retirement account, giving you tax advantages while protecting your wealth from inflation and market downturns.

### Benefits of a Gold IRA
- Tax-advantaged growth (Traditional or Roth options)
- IRS-approved gold stored securely in vaults
- Protection from dollar devaluation
- No home storage risk or security concerns

### How to Open a Gold IRA in 4 Steps
1. Choose an IRS-approved custodian
2. Fund your account (rollover or new deposit)
3. Select IRS-approved gold assets
4. Custodian purchases and stores gold in a secure vault

### Eligible Gold IRA Metals

| Product | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Gold Bars | 99.5% purity+ |
| Gold Coins | Government minted (e.g., American Eagle, Maple Leaf) |

## 4. Buying Scrap Gold Online

### Only Buy Scrap Gold If:
- Seller has 98%+ positive reviews
- You pay near melt value, not retail
- Purity is verified (XRF test recommended later)

### Major Scrap Gold Risks
- Unknown purity
- Misleading weight
- No certificates
- Harder to resell

## How to Avoid Gold Scams Online

### 8 Red Flags
1. Prices far below market value
2. Limited business history
3. No address or customer support
4. No purity verification
5. No insured shipping
6. No buyback guarantee
7. Crypto-only payments
8. Pressure to buy immediately

### 7 Rules to Stay Safe
- Stick to top-rated dealers
- Avoid offers that feel urgent or emotional
- Pay with protected payment methods
- Verify gold authenticity when received
- Store gold securely
- Keep receipts and serial numbers
- Start small with first purchase
